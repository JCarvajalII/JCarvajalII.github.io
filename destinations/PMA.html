Philippine Military Academy

PMA is the premier military institution of the country and is the training ground for future officers of the Armed Forces of the Philippines. Everyone are welcome to explore the grounds, view the cadets performing their drills, and explore the PMA museum and walk around its manicured grounds to see vintage tanks and other historical military weapons.
