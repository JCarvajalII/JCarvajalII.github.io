Teachers Camp

Teachers Camp is located in a beautiful, spacious, campus-like environment , served as a training center and venue for teachers from all over the Philippines who come during the summer break to attend special courses in education. Within the pine-filled camp are classrooms, cottages and dormitories, mess halls and dining areas, administrative offices, plus assembly halls and social centers where many conferences, seminars and other activities are held.
