Lions Head

Along Kennon Road is the undisputed landmark in Baguio where you can take souvenir photograph with the famous lion head which serves as the backdrop. The 40-foot high lion head, carved by a Cordillera artist from a limestone boulder, is a symbol that would proclaim the presence of the Lion Club group in this mountain resort city. Beside the lion head are a few small stalls that sell woodcarving and other native handicraft.
